Welcome To Change Charity Movement

Change Charity Movement was founded by some teenagers wanting to put smile on the faces of children that are not as privileged as them.They have had numerous projects accross the capital city of Accra-Ghana. They organize programs for orphans,donate items to them, organize caros for them and many others.
